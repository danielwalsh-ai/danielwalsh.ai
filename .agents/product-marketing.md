# Product Marketing Context — danielwalsh.ai

Foundation document. Every marketing skill (SEO, copywriting, ads, cold-email, prospecting, etc.) should read this first before doing anything.

## Product

Daniel Walsh runs an independent AI consultancy. He helps established businesses put AI to work as real, owned systems (workflow automation, AI assistants, document processing, reporting pipelines), not pilots and not hype. Builder-led: Daniel designs and builds the systems himself and hands them over documented and running.

Services (fixed price):
- **Strategy & Advisory** — £500 / 60-minute session
- **AI Implementation** — £1,500 / day
- **Training & Workshops** — £1,200 / day
- **Fractional AI Officer** — £5,000 / month (25 hours, 12-month minimum)

Credentials: certified by Google, Oxford and MIT. Independent, with no vendor commissions. UK-based, working worldwide. Free 30-minute discovery call.

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

- **Turnover:** £5m and above.
- **Industries:** Haulage and transport; construction. Close adjacents welcome: logistics, distribution, plant hire, civil engineering, groundworks.
- **Maturity:** Established more than 5 years. Not startups.
- **Buyer:** Direct to the CEO, Managing Director or Owner-Director. A single decision-maker, not a procurement committee.

## Why this ICP fits the product

- £5m+ established operators carry heavy, repetitive, expensive back-office load (compliance paperwork, scheduling, quoting, invoicing, driver and subcontractor admin, management reporting). That is exactly what AI systems remove.
- Haulage, transport and construction are under-served by AI consultancies, most of which chase tech and SaaS clients. Low competition, high receptivity.
- Owner-Directors decide fast and value fixed-price, plain-spoken delivery over jargon and slideware.

## Buyer pains to speak to

- **Key-person dependency:** the business runs in one or two people's heads.
- **Errors and rework** in manual processes (compliance, invoicing, scheduling, quoting).
- **Bottlenecks** that cap growth unless they keep hiring back-office staff.
- **Time lost** to paperwork and reporting instead of winning and delivering work.
- **Scepticism:** they have been sold hype or costly software before that never delivered.

## Positioning statement

For the owner of an established (£5m+) haulage, transport or construction business, Daniel Walsh puts AI to work as fixed-price, fully-owned systems that cut the cost, errors and key-person risk of back-office operations. Built and handed over by someone who understands both the technology and how these businesses actually run, with no vendor commissions and a free discovery call to prove it is worth doing before a penny is spent.

## Differentiators

1. **Builder, not talker.** Builds the systems himself, hands them over documented and running.
2. **Certified** (Google, Oxford, MIT) yet plain-speaking.
3. **Independent.** No vendor kickbacks, so every recommendation serves the client.
4. **Fixed price, plain scope, no surprises.**
5. **Honest.** Will tell a prospect on the free call if AI cannot move the needle for them.

## Channels and plays to run (map to installed marketing skills)

- **prospecting** — build the target list: UK haulage, transport and construction firms, £5m+ turnover, 5+ years, with a reachable Director/CEO.
- **cold-email** — director-level outbound sequences.
- **competitor-profiling / competitors** — map who else sells AI or automation into these verticals.
- **programmatic-seo / content-strategy** — industry landing pages ("AI for haulage firms", "AI for construction companies").
- **ai-seo / seo-audit** — get found by these specific buyers.
- **case studies** — once the first haulage/construction jobs land, they become the strongest sales asset.
